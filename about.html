About Our Company

Driving digital transformation through innovation and expertise

Our Story

Founded in 2015, Portal began as a small team of passionate developers and designers with a shared vision to create impactful digital solutions.

What started as a modest startup has grown into a leading digital agency serving clients across multiple industries, from startups to Fortune 500 companies.

Today, we're proud to have delivered over 200 successful projects and trained thousands of professionals through our courses.

Our Journey

2015 - Foundation

Portal was founded with just 3 team members working from a small office space.

2017 - First Major Client

Landed our first enterprise client, marking our transition to serving larger organizations.

2019 - Expansion

Opened our second office and grew the team to 25 full-time professionals.

2021 - Online Courses Launch

Launched our online learning platform to share our expertise with the wider community.

2023 - Current

Now serving clients in 12 countries with a team of 50+ experts across multiple disciplines.
